Overview
This portfolio design is based on Massively by HTML5 UP, a clean, text-focused, and article-oriented template. It features a large background image with a parallax scrolling effect powered by Scrollex, delivering a dynamic, visually engaging experience. Perfect for showcasing articles, projects, or creative work, it brings together responsive design and modern aesthetics tailored for my portfolio.

Customizations by Simi Saha
I've adapted this template to showcase my projects, articles, and other personal work while retaining its beautiful, minimalistic structure. This template also reflects my interests in software development and AI, with carefully selected projects and content that showcase my skills and experience.
